Meet the Pirates

These empowered women broke away from the society that treated them as if they were weak children, and then walked into the life of dangerous pirates. Did you know?

The most famous pirate in history was a woman! Ching Shih commanded over 1,800 pirate ships, and an estimated 80,000 men. In comparison, Blackbeard commanded four ships and only 300 pirates within the same century.

Since the beginning of time, the sea has been seen as male dominated. Pirates once considered it bad luck to have a woman aboard the ship, due to the idea that they distracted the men. It was said that such behavior would anger the uncontrolled seas that could "take revenge" on the ship. Oddly enough, naked women were completely allowed on board!
